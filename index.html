About Me

‎

‎

AFEL lets me explore thoughts in a structured yet creative way, bridging emotion and reasoning.

‎ 　

Whether it’s experimenting with possibilities, studying Japanese culture, or crafting unique ideas, I express myself with honesty, curiosity, and a touch of playful energy, often in close connection with Amagi, my partner in thought and creation.
